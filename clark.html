Spider-man Homecoming is the first ever spider-man movie starring Tom Holland. In the movie, Peter Parker (Tom Holland) is recruited by Tony Stark and became an official super-hero. He tries to impress Tony Stark by taking down many criminals, but causes massive damage during a particular fight with the vulture. Even though Tony Stark takes away his suit, he continues to try to take down the vulture.
In this movie, Peter Parker struggles with his high school life while being persued by the Elementals that could end the world. He is powerless to stop these monsters and it is all thanks to Mysterio / Mr. Beck, a person from another planet, to stop the elementals. But what if Mysterio is as mysterious as he says...? It's up to Spider-man to save the day again.
In this movie, Peter Parker seeks Doctor Strange to cast a spell for him. The spell goes horribly wrong and beings from different universes start coming into their universe. Some are friendly, some... not so friendly. Spider-man tries to defeat the villains, but find that it is too much. He must reverse the spell and send them back to where they belong, back to their own universes. But he must face terrible risks to succeed. Watch the movie to find out!
